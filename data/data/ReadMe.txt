There are four datasets for four domains where each domain contains 1000 posts. Each post is labeled with either "1" (buying intention/positive class) or "0" (non buying intention/negative class). Please see the details in the paper below.

Zhiyuan Chen, Bing Liu, Meichun Hsu, Malu Castellanos and Riddhiman Ghosh. Identifying Intention Posts in Discussion Forums. To appear in Proceedings of the 2013 Conference of the North American Chapter of the Association for Computational Linguistics: Human Language Technologies (NAACL-HLT 2013), Atlanta, USA, June 09-14, 2013.

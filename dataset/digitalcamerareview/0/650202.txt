fujifilm finepix s5 pro pricing announced .
the finepix s5 pro was announced back in september , but fujifilm has finalized the specifications page and the pricing for this professional level digital slr .
the camera features a super ccd sr pro sensor with `` double pixel '' technology .
the 6 million s-pixels and 6 million r-pixels provide a wider dynamic range than standard single pixel design sensors .
a wider dynamic range means that the sensor can detect a wider range of light and dark values , allowing for pictures that are well exposed without losing details in darker areas of the image .
with the new rp processor pro image processor , the camera can also shoot at sensitivities up to iso 3200 .

you can check each face in a picture with the single push of a button .

today 's show : today we take a look at the western digital wd tv hd media player .
the wd tv hd media player is a device about the size of an external hard drive that plays a/v content from usb storage media .
the wd tv hd media player is optimized for the wd `` my passport '' line of hard drives but it will work with other usb devices .
the player supports 1080p for content and menus navigation .

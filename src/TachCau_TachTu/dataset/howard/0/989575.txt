port blocking and blocking content is illegal with wireless as per crtc ruling .
on june 30th 2010 the crtc ruled on telecom decision crtc 2010 - 445 this means port blocking is illegal .
it is also illegal for wireless providers to slow ( throttle ) time sensitive data like voip or video conferencing ect. .

scholastic and shutterfly collaborate on picture perfect learning arts curriculum .
scholastic and shutterfly today launched the picture perfect learning arts curriculum .
this curriculim is a national , standards - based curriculum gives teachers new ways to build students ' skills in languaged arts through the medium of photos .
the six lessons in the curriculum cover a range of topics , from building classroom communinity and embracing diversity to collaborating on classroom rules and story telling .
the lessons give teachers a way to use digital photography to enforce classroom work .
you can find lessons , including student reproducibles , at www.scholastic.com/shutterfly .
teachers can also create their own classroom photo - sharing web address with shutterfly collections , a free service that lets members create their own web address for picture sharing .
password protection keeps the personalized web address safe and allows for parents , students and teachers to collaborate and share in the classroom experience by adding photos or comments .
parents can also order prints from the collection .
to start a classroom collection or to browse recommended photo activities , visit www.shutterfly.com / inschool
